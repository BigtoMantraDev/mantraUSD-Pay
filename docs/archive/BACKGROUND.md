## **2\. The "Omies 2.0" Strategy (The Pivot)**

Omies has pivoted from a traditional roadmap-based NFT project to a sustainable **DeFi-integrated DAO**. The goal is to solve the "Liquidity Crisis" of NFTs by building a self-sustaining revenue engine that does not rely on secondary royalties.

### **The 5 Pillars of the Ecosystem:**

#### **I. The Validator (The Cash Cow)**

* **What is it?** An official Validator node on the MANTRA Chain.  
* **The Model:** Charges a **5% Commission**.  
* **The Promise:** 100% of the commission revenue goes directly to the **Omies Treasury**.  
* **Purpose:** Provides consistent, recurring monthly revenue to fund operations and sweep (buy back) the NFT floor.

#### **II. The Investment Club (The Product)**

* **What is it?** A suite of custom DeFi vaults accessible via the Omies Dashboard.  
* **Vault A ("Safe Harbor"):** A low-risk, stable-yield vault for **$mantraUSD**. Fees are 0% (Loss Leader strategy) to attract Total Value Locked (TVL).  
* **Vault B ("Alpha Vaults"):** High-risk/high-reward strategies (e.g., Concentrated Liquidity on $OM). Charges a 10-20% performance fee on profits for the Treasury.  
* **Tech:** Uses EVM Pre-compiles to interact with MANTRA's native staking modules.

#### **III. The "Carats" System (The Loyalty Engine)**

* **What is it?** A non-monetary, off-chain loyalty score.  
* **Philosophy:** "Points for Loyalty, not Farming." Carats cannot be sold; they can only be **spent** or **hoarded** for status.  
* **How to Earn:**  
  * **Holding:** Time-weighted multiplier (longer streak \= higher rate).  
  * **Staking:** Depositing assets in Omies Vaults.  
  * **Migration:** Referring friends to move their NFTs from Stargaze to MANTRA.  
* **How to Spend (Burn):**  
  * **Raffles:** Weekly draws for $OM, hardware wallets, or Blue Chip NFTs.  
  * **Governance:** Buying "Board Seats" or boosting voting weight.  
  * **Perks:** Redeeming for SaaS discounts.

#### **IV. Community Games (The Fun Layer)**

* **The Omies Pot:** A "No-Loss Lottery" built on $mantraUSD yield. Users deposit stablecoins, yield is pooled, one winner takes all. Omies holders get a "Ticket Multiplier" (better odds).  
* **The Trading Floor:** A 15-minute "Up/Down" prediction market (e.g., BTC price). The Treasury takes a **2% rake** (House Edge) on every pot.

#### **V. The Perks Portal (Real World Benefits)**

* **What is it?** A token-gated benefit dashboard acting as a "FoundersCard" for crypto.  
* **Offerings:** Exclusive discounts on SaaS tools (TradingView, Koinly), Security (Ledger, VPNs), and other RWA services.  
* **Revenue:** Affiliate commissions from these deals flow back to the Treasury.

## **5\. Migration Context (The "Why")**

Why did we leave Stargaze?

* **Liquidity:** Stargaze is isolated. MANTRA is connected to the massive liquidity of the EVM and RWA markets.  
* **Tech:** Moving to EVM allows for complex DeFi integrations (Vaults, Lending) that were harder to build on Cosmos-Wasm.  
* **Culture:** MANTRA's focus on institutional finance aligns better with the Omies "Investment Club" vision than Stargaze's "Art/Creator" focus.
